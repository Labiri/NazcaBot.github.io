Flexible, data-driven portfolio management

A balanced portfolio is the right blend of careful diversification, a solid model and cold hearted automation. With Nazca's curated market data and powerful strategy customization wizard, you can create an effective long-term investment model and instantly backtest it. Through a trusted exchange, Nazca will automatically allocate your funds in the most efficient way.

Build your long-term portfolio based on data, not emotions.

Diversification and backtesting

The key to a healthy investment portfolio is to properly assess its risk. Nazca's fixed and dynamic asset allocation strategies will ensure proper diversification, in order to estimate and maintain your ideal risk-return ratio. Your results can then be extensively validated and compared to major benchmarks with Nazca's own backtesting engine.

Markets are wild and risky. Tame them with Nazca's data-driven, verifiable approach to diversification.

Clever rebalancing

A straightforward way to maximize the benefits of diversification and maintain your risk level, is to redistribute your funds across the chosen assets when they're out of balance. Nazca's algorithms can redistribute your profits optimally over any time frame, minimizing fees and number of taxable events.

Rebalancing your portfolio is a time consuming, delicate task. Nazca can do it in a magical instant of code execution.

Data Driven

We believe in the power of data. We sort and categorize all available coins, pull live data about them, and display it all in a searchable table.

Risk Management

Every diversification and strategy has risks associated with it, we calculate for you a set of risk measures to give you a clear outlook on what you should expect.

Security First

Nazca never holds your funds, they remain stored on your trusted exchange, and managed with trade-only APIs. Your keys are encrypted and the connection is secured.
